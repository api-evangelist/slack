Slack announced that Slackbot's Model Context Protocol (MCP) client is now generally available, connecting any application to Slackbot through an open standard to unify fragmented software stacks. The launch includes integration with over 20 partner applications, including Amplitude, Atlassian, Box, Canva, Docusign, Gamma, Linear, Miro, Notion, Replit, Webflow, and Zoom, letting users ask Slackbot tasks in plain language while it coordinates actions across platforms and shows results in shared channels.
